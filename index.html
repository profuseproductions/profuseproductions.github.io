About Us

Profuse Productions is Seattle’s premier event production company, delivering top-tier audio, video, and lighting solutions for weddings, corporate gatherings, and cultural celebrations. With specialized expertise in Punjabi and Bollywood entertainment, we create authentic, immersive experiences that resonate deeply with diverse audiences, making every event truly unforgettable.

Founded by Sunny and Aj, passionate audio engineers with a deep love for culture and music, Profuse Productions blends technical precision with creative vision. Our team treats every event as a unique masterpiece, crafting unforgettable vibes through crystal-clear sound and immersive lighting that perfectly bring your vision to life.
